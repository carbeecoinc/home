Privacy Policy for Music Tube

Last Updated: September 1, 2025

This privacy policy describes how CARBEECO SERVICE Company ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application, Music Tube ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) from users of the App. However, we utilize third-party services that may collect certain non-personally identifiable information, as described below:

  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous information about how you use the App. This information may include:
    • App usage statistics (e.g., frequency of use, features used)
    • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system version, screen size)
    • General location information (e.g., country, region) derived from IP address, but not precise location.
    • Events within the App (e.g., button clicks, screens viewed)
  • Firebase In-App Messaging: We use Firebase In-App Messaging to display messages to you within the App. This service may collect data about message delivery and user interaction with those messages.
  • Firebase Messaging: We use Firebase Messaging to send push notifications to your device. For this purpose, Firebase may collect device tokens. You can control push notifications in your device settings.
  • Crashlytics: We use Crashlytics (a Firebase service) to collect crash reports. These reports contain information about your device and the state of the App at the time of a crash, which helps us to diagnose and fix problems. Crash reports do not contain personally identifiable information.
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK: We use the Google Mobile Ads SDK to display advertisements within the App. Google may collect information about your device, your use of the App, and your interests to serve more relevant ads. This information may include:
    • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
    • App usage data
    • Location information
    • Information about other apps you use
    • Demographic and interest information

2. How We Use Information

The information collected by the third-party services is used for the following purposes:

  • To improve the App: We use Firebase Analytics data to understand how users interact with the App, identify areas for improvement, and optimize the user experience.
  • To provide in-app messaging: We use Firebase In-App Messaging to communicate important information and updates to you within the App.
  • To send push notifications: We use Firebase Messaging to send you notifications about new features, updates, or other relevant information, if you have opted in to receive them.
  • To diagnose and fix crashes: We use Crashlytics data to identify and resolve App crashes, ensuring the stability and reliability of the App.
  • To serve advertisements: We use the Google Mobile Ads SDK to display ads within the App, which helps to support the development and maintenance of the App.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any personally identifiable information with third parties. However, the third-party services we use may share the information they collect as described in their respective privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): The information collected by Firebase services (Analytics, In-App Messaging, Messaging, Crashlytics) is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You can find more information about Google's data practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK: The information collected by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is subject to Google's Privacy Policy for advertising. You can find more information about Google's advertising practices here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Data Retention

We do not store any user data. The data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective data retention policies. Please refer to the privacy policies of Google and any other relevant third-party service providers for information about their data retention practices.

5. Your Choices

  • Analytics: You may be able to opt out of certain types of data collection by Firebase Analytics through your device settings or within the App, if such options are provided.
  • Push Notifications: You can control whether or not to receive push notifications by adjusting the notification settings on your device.
  • Advertising: You can control the collection and use of your data for advertising purposes through your device settings. For example, you can limit ad tracking on iOS devices. You can also review and manage your ad settings in your Google account.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address provided below.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post any changes to this privacy policy within the App or notify you by other means. Your continued use of the App after the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
